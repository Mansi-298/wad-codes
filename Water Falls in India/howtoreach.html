How to Reach

Most waterfalls can be reached via train, road or short treks from nearby towns:

  • Dudhsagar: Accessible by train (Castle Rock route) or jeep safari from Goa.
  • Jog Falls: Reachable by bus or car from Shimoga.
  • Athirapally: Well-connected by road from Kochi and Thrissur.
